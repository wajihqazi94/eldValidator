Drivers
This tool only reports on non-historical drivers with one or more ELD settings issues.

User Name
Username should be more than 4 characters long.

Full Name
First Name & Last Name must each be more than 2 characters long.

Authority Information
The authority name and address under the HOS Settings cannot be empty.

Company Information
The company name and address (Home Terminal and Address) under HOS Settings cannot be empty.

DOT Information
Refers to carrier number in the HOS Settings.

License Information
The driver's license number and state (Driver tab) cannot be empty.

The ELD Settings Validator is designed to help administrators view important driver & vehicle information necessary for roadside inspection.

Instructions
  1. Fields marked with an X are an indication that the driver is missing information for that field. Select the information icon on the right hand side for quick access to the driver's User Settings.
  2. Fill in the missing information and select Save at the top of the screen.
  3. For more details on each field below, click on the ? above

FMCSA Offices

These offices are here to answer your questions and to provide you with guidance concerning the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations.
